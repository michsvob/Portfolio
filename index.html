Introduction

Hi! I am years old, come from Prague and nowadays live in Nürnberg. I work as a process engineer in automotive sensor production. In my previous positions I was a process and product engineer at a power semiconductor company and cable manufacturer.

I have a broad range of skills, experiences and interests. Explore this website to learn more about me and my projects and contact me if you are interested in collaboration!
